Traditional Mediterranean Recipes

The Mediterranean diet is renowned for its health benefits and delicious flavors, emphasizing fresh ingredients, olive oil, and herbs.

Classic Greek Tzatziki:
Ingredients:
- 2 cups Greek yogurt
- 1 large cucumber, grated and drained
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon fresh dill
- Salt to taste

Instructions:
1. Grate cucumber and drain excess water using paper towels
2. Mix yogurt with garlic, olive oil, and lemon juice
3. Fold in cucumber and dill
4. Season with salt and chill for 30 minutes

Italian Caprese Salad:
Ingredients:
- 4 large tomatoes, sliced
- 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
- Fresh basil leaves
- Extra virgin olive oil
- Balsamic vinegar
- Sea salt and black pepper

Instructions:
1. Arrange alternating slices of tomato and mozzarella
2. Tuck basil leaves between slices
3. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar
4. Season with salt and pepper

Spanish Gazpacho:
Ingredients:
- 6 ripe tomatoes
- 1 cucumber
- 1 red bell pepper
- 1 small red onion
- 2 cloves garlic
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons sherry vinegar
- Salt and pepper

Instructions:
1. Roughly chop all vegetables
2. Blend until smooth
3. Strain if desired for finer texture
4. Chill for 2 hours before serving
5. Garnish with diced vegetables and olive oil

These recipes showcase the fresh, simple approach that makes Mediterranean cuisine both healthy and flavorful.
